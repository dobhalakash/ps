Coupons

Discount coupons are created and managed centrally by the PS Sports platform team and apply storewide across all sellers. If you'd like to propose a promotional coupon for your products, please contact the PS Sports support team.
How Coupons Work
  • Customers can apply a coupon code at checkout for a discount on their order subtotal.
  • Coupons can be percentage-based or a flat amount, with optional minimum order value and usage limits.
  • Discounts are applied before tax and shipping are calculated.
